Oatmeal Pancakes

Description

This recipe is one from my mom, and is a Sunday morning classic!
The soaked oats add a bit of texture and flavour that (in my opinion) elevates these above buttermilk pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup whole-wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 Tsp. cinnamon
  • 1 Tbsp. brown sugar
  • 1 Tbsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 Tsp. salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup oil or melted butter

Steps

  1. Combine oats and milk in bowl, leave for 10 minutes to soak.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, sift together dry ingredients. (Everything except eggs, oil, oats)
  3. In a seperate bowl, combine eggs, oil or butter, oats and milk.
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry, and mix into a smooth batter.
  5. Heat pan (I like cast iron) or griddle over medium heat. Add butter or oil to grease pan.
  6. Add pancake batter in 1/4 cup amounts. Cook on one side until bubbles appear, then flip and cook for ~2 minutes.
    Cooking time will vary with pan type, heat, batter amount, etc. You'll figure it out!
