Red Lentil Soup

Description

A delicious and healthy comfort food, this red lentil soup makes for a great weeknight dinner. It uses split red lentils, which cook quickly and break down for a great texture. Taken from The Feed Zone cookbook.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 small onion, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon minced jalapeno, or 2 tablespoons Sambal
  • 1 cup coconut milk (I often use whole 450ml can)
  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  • 3 cups water or stock (I prefer vegetable stock)

Toppings

  • Plain yogurt
  • Diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger

Steps

  1. In a large stock pot, heat oil over medium heat. Add onions and cook until just soft. Add garlic, jalapeno and curry powder, cook for ~1 minute, or until fragrant. Add coconut milk and let cook for 5 minutes.
  2. Add lentils and stock. Bring to boil, then turn heat down and simmer until lentils are tender, about 20-30 minutes.
  3. Add desired toppings, serve, and enjoy!
